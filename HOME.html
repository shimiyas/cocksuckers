ABOUT THERESIAN

Theresian School of Cavite is located at Agueda Lane, Tirona Highway, Habay 1, Bacoor City, Cavite. It was established in 1988 and it was formerly known as the Kiddie Kollege of Cavite. The school was originaly meant to cater and teach the children of habay, bacoor, cavite and there were only a few facilities but over the years the school has developed and even caters students of K-12. Theresian School of Cavite is also a well known institute not only in its academics but also its sports.
